Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-26

Overview

We keep things simple. This website is static and currently does not use forms or user accounts. We don’t sell personal information.

What We Collect

  • Server logs: Our hosting provider (Netlify) may collect standard logs (IP address, browser, pages visited) for security and performance.
  • Map tiles: The “Find Us” map uses OpenStreetMap tiles via third-party providers; their servers receive your IP when tiles load.

Cookies & Analytics

We are not using analytics or advertising cookies at this time. If that changes, we’ll update this policy and the site will show a cookie notice where required.

Your Choices

You can block cookies and third-party content in your browser settings. You may contact us to request access to or deletion of any information you’ve submitted directly to us.

Children & Age Restriction

This site is for adults 21+. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.
